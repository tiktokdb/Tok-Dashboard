Privacy Policy

Effective:

TokBoard is a creator workflow tool that connects to your Google account (with your consent) to read/write rows in Google Sheets that you control. We don’t run a database for your product data—your rows live in your Google account.

What we collect

  • Google account email (read-only), used to identify your session.
  • OAuth tokens in your browser only, to access Google Sheets/Drive.file on your behalf.
  • Payment info is processed by our payment provider (e.g., Stan Store). TokBoard never sees full card data.
  • Basic product analytics and error logs (aggregated, non-sensitive).

Where your data lives

Your product rows are stored in Google Sheets within your Google account. We do not store a server copy of your sheet contents.

How we use data

  • To let you sign in and use TokBoard.
  • To read/write the specific spreadsheet tabs you direct us to.
  • To communicate essential updates (e.g., billing or service notices).
  • To prevent abuse and improve reliability.

Google API Services

Use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Payments

Subscriptions are handled by a third-party processor (e.g., Stan Store). They act as the merchant of record for your payment details. TokBoard receives only minimal billing metadata (e.g., success/failed status, plan, email).

Cookies & local storage

We use local storage/cookies for sign-in state and basic preferences. You can clear them in your browser at any time.

Access & deletion

  • Revoke Google access anytime at myaccount.google.com/permissions.
  • Delete your spreadsheet from Google Drive to remove your product data.
  • For billing records or account questions, email us (see Contact).

Children

TokBoard is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

International users

Your data may be processed where our providers operate. By using TokBoard you consent to such processing.

Changes

We may update this policy. We’ll adjust the effective date and, if material, notify you in-app or by email.
